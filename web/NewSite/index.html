Welcome to the Computer Science Faculty/Staff Tracker


We provide an effective tool that will allow students to be able to easily determine when and where they can meet with the faculty and staff at North Carolina Agricultural and Technical State University. The faculty and staff are able to add, modiy, and delete their available hours and locations to the system. The students can then view all of the provided schedules.
